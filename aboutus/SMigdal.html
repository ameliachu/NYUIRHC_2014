Sharon Migdal

Vice President of Events

Hey IRHC! My name is Sharon Migdal and I’m absolutely thrilled to be serving as Vice President of Events on this year’s Executive Board! I am currently a sophomore double majoring in Early Childhood Education and Speech Pathology. I have an undying love for Quest Bars, cereal, funny GIFS, the Wobble and fancy perfumes. (In other words, if you get me any of these items I will love you forever.) IRHC became a second home to me at the beginning of my freshman year when I joined Weinstein’s Hall Council (W.H.A.T.!) as the Director of Events. After seeing the impact of our council’s actions on one residence hall, I decided to start attending IRHC’s weekly meetings and fell in love with the organization. Being a part of every student’s residential experience is unbelievable and incredibly rewarding, not to mention all the best people are involved in Res Life here at NYU. (wink wink) Through IRHC, I’ve gained a wonderful family and I can’t wait to see all the fun and madness that this year has in store for us!
